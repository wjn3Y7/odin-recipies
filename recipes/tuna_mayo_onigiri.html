tuna mayo onigiri

recipe from https://www.contemplatingsweets.com/tuna-mayo-onigiri/

Description

Onigiri is one of the most recognizable Japanese snacks there is. From homemade lunches to convenience stores, onigiri can be found just about anywhere.
The Japanese rice ball is really simple. Rice is shaped in (usually) a triangle and filled with some sort of salty or sour filling.
What Iâ€™m showing you today: spicy tuna onigiri filling made with Japanese mayo.

Ingredients

  • japanese short grain rice
  • canned tuna
  • soy sauce
  • white sugar
  • mirin
  • japanese mayonnaise
  • salt
  • water

Steps

  1. prepare rice
  2. add the can of tuna to a small pan, and place over medium heat.
    In a small bowl, combine sugar, soy sauce, and mirin, and pour over the tuna. Cook and stir until the sauce is fully absorbed by the tuna.
  3. spread out a square of plastic wrap, and put some salt on it.
  4. place a scoop of rice onto the wrap, and spread it out a bit.
    add some tuna and top with a little dollop of mayonnaise.
  5. Wrap the rice up so that it completely surrounds the fillings, shape it into a tight triangle
